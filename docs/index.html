Privacy Policy for LOKEY Password Vault

Last updated: November 2025

LOKEY Password Vault (“the App”) is designed to securely store your credentials locally on your device. We do not collect, store, transmit, or have access to any personal data or passwords you save in the App.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

The App does not collect any personal information, credentials, analytics data, or usage statistics. All data remains on your device and is fully under your control.

2. Local-Only Data Storage

All credentials and notes you save are stored locally and securely on your device using Apple’s Keychain encryption. None of this data leaves your device.

3. Device Security & Biometrics

The App may use biometric authentication (Face ID or Touch ID) to unlock access. This authentication is handled entirely by Apple’s Secure Enclave. We do not have access to your biometric data.

4. No Third-Party Integrations

The App does not use third-party analytics, tracking services, advertising networks, or external servers.

5. Your Rights

Because the App does not collect or store personal data outside your device, you retain full control of all information. You may delete the App at any time to permanently remove all stored data.
