Why LOKEY

Designed for people who don't want their passwords in the cloud.

LOKEY is intentionally minimal. No sign-ups, no sync servers, no telemetry – just a fast, focused place to keep the sensitive details you can’t afford to lose.

  • Biometric lock. Unlock your vault with Face ID or Touch ID. Authentication is handled entirely by Apple’s Secure Enclave.
  • On-device keychain storage. Credentials are stored using Apple Keychain APIs – encrypted and sandboxed on your device.
  • Smart categories. Group logins by Social, Finance, Work or your own custom categories and filter them in a single tap.
  • Secure clipboard. Copy usernames and passwords with a tap; LOKEY keeps clipboard usage intentional and lightweight.
  • Dark-mode ready. A clean, monochrome interface that looks at home on modern iPhones in both light and dark themes.

Quick FAQ
Do you ever see my passwords?
No. All credentials are stored locally on your iPhone using Apple Keychain. They never leave your device.
Does LOKEY require an account?
No accounts, logins, or subscriptions are required. Install the app, set it up with biometrics, and start adding entries.
How do I delete my data?
Because everything is stored on-device, deleting the app from your iPhone will remove all saved credentials.
